Design is so much more than making things pretty. My design work is focused on making things usable: function over form. I love to make sense of complex information systems. I'm happiest when working in a combination of both strategic and tactical design work.

## Current position

In September 2024, I slightly change careers. Although still working at the Dutch provider of employee insurance schemes, [UWV](https://www.uwv.nl/), I'm now responsible for training and coaching of colleagues to increase their level of digital literacy.

## Certification

I have a bachelor in Media, Information and Communication (2016, University of Applied Science Amsterdam). In the 80ies, I was trained as a graphical designer and engineer. The early design computers were code based, which was very similar to HTML. I have a basic+ level knowledge of HTML and CSS (mostly selftaught). I am a certified content designer.

I'm a continuous learner. If you want to learn more about User Experience Design, [I highly recommend the Interaction Design Foundation courses](https://www.interaction-design.org/learn-ux-design?r=monique-dubbelman) (referral link: this means I get 3 months subscription for free).

Recently, I started embracing the use of AI (I'm so sorry!) more and more and really enjoy being able to create whatever I want. I mainly use it to create course outlines and code apps.

## UX Consultancy

For 3 years, I was part of the international design collective UXATT. Together with Cathi Bosco and Jackie D'Elia, I helped business owners, developers, agencies, product teams and others in the digital space improve the usability and accessibility of their product.

### Work for UXATT

- User research, UX design, accessibility consultancy and prototyping for various [WooCommerce plugins by Kathy Darling](https://www.kathyisawesome.com/).
- User research, UX design, content design, accessibility testing for [GravityView](https://gravityview.co/), a WordPress plugin that creates content from form submissions.
- For [GoDaddy](https://www.godaddy.com/), an international hosting company, we created prototypes and did a usability study to research options for a client portal and onboarding in WordPress.
- Full redesign of a [custom webshop and elearning platform](https://members.iab-ingenieurs.nl/) for IAB Ingenieurs. Included prototyping, creating a UI kit, project management and hand off to developers.

### Past design and consultancy work

As an independent designer, I have helped various small business owners to identify their online goals with strategy workshops and design consultancy. I've guided businesses, NGO's and other organisations on branding, design, communication and content strategy.
