## Cultural

Due to some kind of flu marathon, I didn't go to any movie in January and February. But we picked up our habit of going on a nearly weekly basis and we saw Truffaut's classic Jim et Jules. We also saw some great photo exhibitions in Huis Marseille in Amsterdam. Which is, besides a great museaum, a wonderful double old canal building. Worth a visit for multiple reasons!

## Health
So flu happened to me. I had a first time experience of high fever. Luckily only for one evening, but I prefer it to keep the usual distance again for the coming years, thank you.

## Work

It's great teaching people quick wins to work smarter with their digital toolkit. I enjoy the tiny light bulbs appearing when they discover something useful. Aside from online training we've also started visiting offices around the country to give workshops. We have to find the right mix of timely communication and the right offer that pulls colleagues away from their daily occupance, but it's appreciated that we get out of HQ.

## Garden

Spring is happening! All the magnolia's and first spring blossom look amazing against the clear blue sky this week. I went to our community garden/allotment and the birds were singing beautifully.

## Hobby
